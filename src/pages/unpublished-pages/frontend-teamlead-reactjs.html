Frontend teamlead (React.JS) for an international blockchain startup that develops tools for Banks and Funds to use blockchain & tokens and comply with the SEC and other regulators. You'll need to join a small team of React.JS developers, dive deep into the blockchain specificity and scale team to be able to develop several big React.JS apps in parallel.

Our team spread all over the world, the tech team is located in Saint-Petersburg. The tech team consists of about 20 senior developers with 5+ years of experience, many of them worked for well-known IT companies (Yandex, Lazada, Mera, etc).

We are currently working on two B2C products that target large existing markets. Also, we have established relationships with several notable financial institutions to work on the integration of blockchain technology into the existing enterprise infrastructure.

Investors are from USA, Europe, Asia and Middle East, start-up is fully funded for 3 years of development.

What you'll be doing:

  • Manage a team of frontend developers
  • Develop big fronted apps using React.JS tech stack
  • Integrate apps with various blockchain protocols
  • Develop open-source projects (React libraries for blockchain)

What we expect:

  • experience in managing teams of front-end developers
  • deep knowledge and proficiency with React.JS, UI, front-end architecture, modern libraries
  • js & css3 & html5
  • webpack & gulp/grunt
  • experience with at least one other frontend framework: angular, vue.js, ember etc.
  • experience with different markup approaches: BEM/RSCSS/etc
  • experience with complex front-end UI, including complex API interactions
  • strong development culture: testing, code review, CI/CD, etc.
  • self-motivated professional able to work independently
  • fluent Russian, upper-intermediate English

Benefits:

  • Introduction to the blockchain from scratch
  • High salaries set in USD, permanent contract (RF Labor Codex), 100% white salary
  • Full-time, office in the city center with flexible hours
  • Relocation package is provided for candidates from other regions
  • Bonuses, Medical insurance
  • Latest powerful MacBook and a big display
