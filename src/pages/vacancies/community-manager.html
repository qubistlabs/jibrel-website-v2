Company Overview

Jibrel is a blockchain development company, with offices in Zug, Switzerland, New York, US and Saint Petersburg, Russia. Jibrel focuses on building financial technology products for Tier 1 Banks and non-bank financial institutes as well as governments and regulators. Jibrel aims to leverage the latest innovations in distributed ledger and smart contract technology to build the financial networks of the future.

Overview of Responsibilities

  • Moderate user-generated content accordingly for each platform we engage on (Telegram, Twitter, Facebook, Medium, Reddit etc)
  • Craft compelling positioning and messaging narratives across different channels (Telegram, Twitter, Facebook, Medium, Reddit etc) and match the message accordingly for each platform
  • Work with the Jibrel team to get major announcements out on all platforms in a timely manner (including translation vendor management)
  • Respond to community queries, and collect feedback from the community to help enhance our product
  • Liaise with country community managers who moderate non-English forums (such as WeChat and KakaoTalk)

Required Work & Academic Experience

  • Experience launching community initiatives (e.g. building an online forum, launching an ambassador program, creating an event series and writing an email newsletter, etc.)
  • Ability to use data and metrics to measure progress and create new campaigns and strategy
  • Excellent social and networking skills, with the ability to influence, persuade and negotiate
  • Experience creating content that drives engagement and lead generation
  • Passion for or knowledge of the blockchain and cryptocurrency space
  • Record of participation in different cryptocurrency social outlets such as Telegram, Reddit, Twitter, or Medium

Compensation & Perks

  • Competitive salary based on work experience
  • Remuneration package including equity and tokens
  • Unlimited Paid Time Off

Our team is international, meritocratic and growing rapidly. We value strong self-starters and team players who are excited about building out the global financial services infrastructure of the future.
