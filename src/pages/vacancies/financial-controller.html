Company Overview

Jibrel is a blockchain development company, with offices in Zug, Switzerland, New York, US and Saint Petersburg, Russia. Jibrel focuses on building financial technology products for Tier 1 Banks and non-bank financial institutes as well as governments and regulators. Jibrel aims to leverage the latest innovations in distributed ledger and smart contract technology to build the financial networks of the future.

Overview of Responsibilities

  • Manage annual audit review and tax preparations, create and maintain financial models for the company, and monitor department spend and budget
  • Oversee all global accounting functions and ensure that record keeping meets the requirements of auditors and government agencies
  • Set KPI on controlling systems across departments, develop tools for automation and monthly reports, and present to leadership team
  • Work with leadership to create business cases and forecast financial impacts, including setting revenue and profitability strategy

Required Work & Academic Experience

  • Experience in public accounting and/or a technology organization
  • Knowledge of automated accounting / bookkeeping / auditing systems across countries and different entities preferred
  • Experience in creating business plans and financial models
  • Passion for or knowledge of the blockchain and cryptocurrency space
  • Bachelor’s degree in finance or accounting or equivalent practical experience
  • Financial Services industry experience a plus

Compensation & Perks

  • Competitive salary based on work experience
  • Remuneration package including equity and tokens
  • Unlimited Paid Time Off

Our team is international, meritocratic and growing rapidly. We value strong self-starters and team players who are excited about building out the global financial services infrastructure of the future.
