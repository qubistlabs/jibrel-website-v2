Jibrel is a looking for a Marketing & PR Director to join its New York office. Jibrel's Marketing & PR director will oversee all external communication and outward facing initiatives, including Digital Marketing, Public Relations, Growth Marketing and User Acquisition.

Role & Responsibilities

  • Define Jibrel's product and technology marketing plan and messaging for a wide array of audiences that have varying levels of knowledge and exposure across financial services or blockchain technology
  • Work with the business team to ensure PR narratives align with internal strategies
  • Oversee brand, content, and earned media programs for engagement and follower growth in Jibrel’s social media channels
  • Oversee all public relations, external category communications, and announcements for the company
  • Manage PR and marketing vendors and partners, including relationship with target publications
  • Leverage existing media relationships and cultivate new contacts within business and industry media
  • Manage an editorial calendar for all external content releases and PR initiatives
  • Monitor, analyze, and communicate marketing and PR results to leadership

Requirements

  • High comfort for navigating an undefined category and press landscape
  • Experience leading and and implementing marketing/communication strategies at high-growth companies
  • Genuine passion for and knowledge of the blockchain and cryptocurrency space
  • Bachelor’s degree (English, Communications, Marketing or related disciplines preferred) or equivalent practical experience

Compensation & Benefits

  • Competitive salary based on work experience
  • Generous bonuses (cash, equity and tokens)
  • Flexible working hours and option to work remotely
  • Option to work from other offices (Zug, New York, Saint Petersburg, Dubai)
  • Generous relocation package, based on location
  • Health Benefits (Medical Insurance)
  • Latest and greatest Macbook Pro
  • Introduction to blockchain from scratch
