Jibrel is hiring a full-time marketer based in Dubai, UAE. The company is looking for a fully bilingual (Arabic and English) marketing generalist with at least 2 years of relevant work experience, excellent writing skills, a success record in social media and content marketing, and a passion for blockchain and fintech.

Jibrel is a promising and fully-funded international startup focused on developing blockchain solutions for financial services. The company currently provides both business-to-consumer and business-to-business offerings. Jibrel has a very experienced development team with a track record of building enterprise solutions. In addition, Jibrel’s business team has a strong record of working with Tier 1 financial institutions across North America, Europe, Asia, the Middle East, and North Africa. 

The Jibrel platform [www.jibrel.com](www.jibrel.com) enables startups and investors to connect, in previously unimaginable ways, to create capital formation. Global investors can access investment opportunities in the Middle East and North Africa through a regulated equity management platform. Conversely, Jibrel allows startups to attract, onboard and manage a much larger and more diversified pool of investors. This role will focus primarily on the Jibrel platform. 

## Requirements

* Demonstrated track record of working on social media and community management (e.g. Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn)
* Native Arabic speaker with strong spoken and written fluency in Modern Standard Arabic and English
* Able to work full time out of Jibrel’s Dubai office
* Ability to travel up to 25% of the time (Gulf and other MENA countries as needed) 
* Visual design & video editing: able to use Sketch, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Premier, iMovie, and/or similar software to generate infographics, social media cards, animated explainer videos, and related visual and video content

## Preferred Skills

* Prior work experience with MENA region startups, with demonstrated background and interest in fintech/financial services
* B.A. in marketing, communications, language arts, design, or a related field

## Core Duties

* Post daily content across social media 
* Lead community management and social media efforts and foster engagement (viral/earned media) across platforms
* Foster engagement (viral marketing / earned media) across social platforms
* Generate and manage English and Arabic content (articles, infographics, videos, social cards, etc.) 
* Assist in running email marketing systems for transactional and promotional emails 
* Write articles on Jibrel, and on startups, investing, tokenization, and other related topics
* Engage the online MENA community through contests, webinars, etc. 
* Support regional event-marketing initiatives
* Measure success of earned and owned media efforts, and optimize based on data

## Compensation, Benefits & Perks

* Competitive salary 
* Performance-based quarterly bonuses
* Comprehensive insurance coverage (health, dental, vision)
* MacBook
* Flexible working hours

If interested, please send your resume, two writing samples (one each in English & Arabic), and visual/video design samples to [careers@jibrel.network](mailto:careers@jibrel.network).
