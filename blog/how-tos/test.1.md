## Jibrel – Bringing the benefits of blockchain to the world of commodity trading 
Jibrel was recently accepted into the Gulf Bonds and Sukuk Association (GBSA). This is a great milestone in our journey and vision of putting currencies, equities, commodities and other financial assets on the Ethereum blockchain. Not only does the acceptance aid in solidifying our foothold in the Middle East, but it provides the opportunity to create better products and have access to key institutional players. In this article we will explore what the Gulf Bonds and Sukuk Association is and how it will play an instrumental role for Jibrel moving forward.

### What is the Gulf Bonds and Sukuk Association

The Gulf Bonds and Sukuk Association is a Middle Eastern organization that is focused on promoting liquidity and prosperity in fixed income markets. The organization was founded in 2010 and has members from all parts of the Middle Eastern debt industry ecosystem. This inclusive approach allows issuers, investors, banks, ratings agencies, legal firms and service providers to benefit by having better access to one another. The GBSA does this through specialized regional committees, working groups and national chapters across the region that address high priority issues proposed by its members. As debt capital markets are increasingly relied upon and GCC markets are integrated into the global emerging market universe, the GBSA’s role is growing as well.

### What This Means For Jibrel?

This acceptance would help Jibrel gain access to insights and a network of diverse players within the Middle Eastern debt market. Such access would help optimize offerings and create products that are not only in tune with the needs of the current market, but the needs of tomorrow as well. Jibrel would be able to better understand the main pain points of existing processes and design smart contracts and products accordingly.

The GBSA also assists companies in communication with central banks, governments and regulators. Such assistance is crucial in an industry where regulation and compliance are around every corner. This will serve as another way for Jibrel to open a dialogue with bank, treasury and DCM (debt capital market) teams. The meetings and conferences held by the organization would ensure Jibrel’s voice is heard throughout the region as we forge ties with key institutions and regulators.
